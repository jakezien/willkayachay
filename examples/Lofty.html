Here's how it works.

Scan

A professional from our staff scans your space with a proprietary, next-generation 3D camera.

Upload

As soon as scanning is complete, the data is processed into a model and uploaded to our servers.

View & Share

Your space is now available as an interactive 3D model, viewable by anyone with whom you share the link.
